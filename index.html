01

With its experience in the field, Naz is positioned to be a catalyst towards a world where equality and social justice is accessible to all, by transforming individuals into agents of change. The rebranding project for the Naz Foundation aims to capture the organization’s essence with a modern and cohesive design identity. The project aims to establish a renewed visual identity that resonates with its audience and purpose.

02

This project involves creating a filmographic poster for the movie Divergent, focusing on its core themes and narrative essence. The design aims to visually capture the spirit of the story while highlighting its unique elements in a creative and impactful way. Through careful attention to detail and symbolism, the poster serves as a tribute to the film’s dynamic world and compelling characters.

03

This project focuses on solving the challenges of low visitor engagement and language accessibility at the Savarkar Smarak; by identifying the core issues—language barriers and lack of interactive marketing strategies. The solutions aim to bridge communication gaps, provide multilingual translations, and create an engaging experience for visitors. The project emphasizes user-centric design to enhance cultural understanding, promote inclusivity, and inspire deeper exploration of the Smarak’s rich heritage.

04

GeoFlow is a bold and modern typeface that blends geometric precision with fluid, organic curves. Its unique cut-out shapes and dynamic lines create a striking visual balance between structure and movement. Ideal for creative projects, branding, and display use, GeoFlow stands out with its distinct, artistic character, making it perfect for capturing attention in both print and digital mediums. Whether you're looking to make a statement or add a creative flair, this font brings a fresh, contemporary edge to any design.
